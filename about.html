About Company

Founded in 1997, Maruti Industries began as a technical solution provider for steel and tube industries. Over time, we evolved into a trusted manufacturer of Special Purpose Machines (SPMs) and material handling systems, tailored to the unique needs of the tube industry. Backed by a skilled team of engineers and operators, we deliver customized automation solutions that enhance productivity, safety, and precision.
