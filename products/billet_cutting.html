Billet Cutting Machine

Salient Features
Compact and effective mechanism for billet cutting operations.
Billet cutting torch adjustable to suit various billet diameters.
AC motor with Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) for speed control.
Speed range: 10 to 45 rpm depending on billet size.
Operator-friendly layout with smooth cutting cycle.
Sturdy base frame for vibration-free operation.
Billet Cutting Rate
75 mm Ø billet 70 cuts/hr
125 mm Ø billet 60 cuts/hr
150 mm Ø billet 55 cuts/hr
175 mm Ø billet 45 cuts/hr
200 mm Ø billet 35 cuts/hr
Functional Overview
Designed for high-speed billet cutting with minimal operator effort.
VFD allows precise control over cutting speed based on billet diameter.
Torch positioning system ensures clean and accurate cuts.
Ideal for integration with billet conveyors and rotary hearth furnaces.
